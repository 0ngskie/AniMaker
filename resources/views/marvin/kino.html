Kino no Tabi
Score: 8.31 | Year: 2003 | Genre: Action, Adventure, Slice of Life

The story follows Kino, a young adventurer who travels the world on a talking motorcycle named Hermes. They explore the people and cultures of different places, staying only three days at each location. The series is known for its philosophical themes and the unique perspective of the main character, Kino. The series is episodic, with each episode featuring a different country and its customs.
